## See it in action

The **Catalog** is home: your scanned library as preview cards, searchable
and grouped by designer and release.

![The Plinth catalog: a scanned library browsed as preview cards, grouped by designer and release](/screenshots/catalog.png)

The **Render studio** orients a multi-part mini and renders a studio
preview through Blender — the same locked look used for batch renders.

![Render studio: orient a multi-part mini and render a studio preview through Blender](/screenshots/render-studio.png)

The **Base Cutter** builds landscape bases — pick a terrain, scatter
forest-floor debris, lay out your minis, and cut their bases to fit.

![Base Cutter: generate a landscape base with scattered terrain and cut models to fit](/screenshots/base-cutter.png)
